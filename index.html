About Me

I'm Rudy Crespo, a full-stack web developer from New York City bent on making the digital universe a more pulchritudinous place one website at a time. After graduating from Fordham University with a B.A. in Philosophy, I attended General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, where I learned to spin magic in the back-end and create transcendent user interfaces on the front-end.

In my free time I enjoy gormandizing, questioning reality, writing, playing basketball, watching quirky videos on Vimeo, reading graphic novels, listening to hip-hop, and hanging out with my family.
