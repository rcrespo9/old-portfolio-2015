About Me

Who I Am

I'm Rudy Crespo, a full-stack web developer from New York City bent on making the digital universe a more pulchritudinous place one website at a time. With Ruby on Rails as my dynamic and trustworthy sidekick, I can spin magic in the back-end and create transcendent user interfaces on the front-end.

In 2012, I graduated from Fordham University with a B.A. in Philosophy. A year later, I had the pleasure of attending General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, where I learned the tricks of this marvelous trade.

In my free time I enjoy gormandizing, guzzling coffee, questioning reality, frequenting art galleries, pumping iron, playing basketball, watching indie movies and quirky videos on Vimeo, reading, writing, and spending time with my family.
