About Me

Who I Am

I'm Rudy Crespo, a full-stack web developer from New York City bent on making the digital universe a more pulchritudinous place one website at a time.

With Ruby on Rails as my dynamic and trustworthy sidekick, I can spin magic in the back-end and create transcendent user interfaces on the front-end.


Education

In 2012, I graduated from Fordham University with a B.A. in Philosophy. A year later, I had the pleasure of attending General Assembly's Web Development Immersive, where I learned the tricks of this marvelous trade.


Fun Facts

In my free time I enjoy gormandizing, guzzling coffee, questioning reality, frequenting art galleries, pumping iron, playing basketball, watching indie movies and quirky videos on Vimeo, reading, writing (you can check out some of my work here), and spending time with my family.
